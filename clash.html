Clever Clash

Test your knowledge and quick thinking in the ultimate brain challenge!

Description:

Clever Clash is a team-based event that tests participants' knowledge in technology and engineering, covering everything from core computer science to emerging tech. The quiz includes diverse question types, challenging critical thinking, problem-solving, and quick decision-making under time pressure. It's an opportunity to showcase teamwork, strategy, and intellectual potential in a competitive environment.

Instructions:

Round 1: Technical Quiz

  1. Each team must consist of 2 members.
  2. Teams must answer each question within the specified time limit.
  3. The questions will cover technology related questions.
  4. Teams will be ranked based on accuracy and time taken to answer the questions. The top-performing teams will be shortlisted for Round 2.

Round 2: Connection Game (Technology-based Challenge)

  1. Teams will be presented with clues, images, or keywords related to technology and computer science.
  2. Teams must identify connections between these elements and deduce the correct answers within the time limit.
  3. The team that identifies the most correct connections and answers within the time limit will be crowned the winner of Clever Clash.
