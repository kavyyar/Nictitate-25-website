TechNova

Showcase your research and innovative ideas through an engaging paper presentation.

Description:

TechNova is a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas, research, and technological advancements through paper presentations. Participants will present their technical papers on emerging technologies, engineering solutions, or scientific advancements, followed by a Q&A session with judges.

Instructions:

  1. . Participants can register individually or in teams (maximum of 2 members). . Multiple teams from the same institution are allowed. The paper must be original and plagiarism-free (similarity index < 15%).
  2. Submit the paper in PDF format before the deadline.
  3. . Time limit: 8 minutes for the presentation + 2 minutes for Q&A. Presentation slides must be in PPT/PDF format.
  4. Use a clear and structured format (Introduction, Problem Statement, Methodology, Results, Conclusion, Future Scope). All presentations must be in English.
  5. Participants must join at least 15 minutes before their scheduled presentation. Any form of plagiarism or copied content will lead to disqualification.
  6. Last-minute changes in paper or presentation will not be allowed. Judges' decisions are final and binding.
