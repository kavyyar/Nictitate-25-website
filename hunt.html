Mystic Hunt

Embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover hidden treasures!

Description:

Gear up for Mystic Hunt an electrifying treasure hunt filled with mind-boggling riddles, cryptic clues, and thrilling challenges! Navigate through multiple checkpoints, crack the codes, and race against time to uncover the grand treasure. Only the sharpest minds and quickest feet will triumph!

Instructions:

  1. Teams of 2-4 members. Solo participation not allowed. The hunt consists of multiple levels with clues leading to the next checkpoint.
  2. Teams must solve each clue correctly before proceeding. No use of mobile phones, internet, or external help—only your wits! Tampering or damaging clues leads to immediate disqualification.
  3. The team that reaches the final treasure first wins. In case of a tie, the team with the least time taken wins. Follow instructions from organizers at all times.
  4. No pushing, running in restricted areas, or unfair play. Judges’ decisions are final and binding.
