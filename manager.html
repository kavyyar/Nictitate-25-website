The Maestro

Demonstrate leadership, problem-solving, and decision-making in a fast-paced challenge!

Description:

The Maestro event challenges participants' managerial, analytical, and creative thinking skills. Teams will receive a random topic on the spot, brainstorm for 2 minutes, and present their ideas. The jury will evaluate critical thinking, communication, and innovation. This event offers a platform to showcase creativity, problem-solving, and quick thinking under pressure..

Instructions:

  1. Each team must consist of 2 members.
  2. The topic will be given on the spot.
  3. Teams will have only 2 minutes to prepare and present.
  4. Each team will get 2 minutes to present their ideas
  5. The presentation can be verbal only.
