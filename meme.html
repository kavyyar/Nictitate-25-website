Memeocalypse

Unleash your creativity in the Meme Creation Challenge!

Description:

Memeocalypse is a high-energy on-the-spot meme creation battle where teams of two go head-to-head in a test of wit, humor, and creativity.

Instructions:

  1. 1-2 members per team. extra members are not allowed.
  2. The theme will be announced at the beginning of the event. pre-made memes are not allowed.
  3. Teams will have 1 hour to design and submit their meme. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  4. Memes must be original and created on the spot using provided tools.
  5. Each team must submit their meme within the given time frame to the event organizers.
