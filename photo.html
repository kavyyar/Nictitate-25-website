Shot sparks

Create captivating stories through visuals in the Short Film Challenge!

Description:

ShotSpark is a short film competition that ignites creativity and storytelling. Participants showcase their filmmaking skills through engaging short films that captivate, inspire, and entertain. The competition welcomes diverse genres, including fiction, documentary, animation, and experimental films.

Instructions:

  1. Teams can have 1-5 members. Film duration: 3 to 10 minutes (including credits). Must be original and copyright-free (no stock footage/music unless licensed).
  2. Any language is allowed, but English subtitles are mandatory. The film must be in MP4 format, 1080p resolution. Films must align with the given theme (if any).
  3. No explicit, offensive, or politically sensitive content. Plagiarism leads to disqualification.
  4. Submit via Google Drive/WeTransfer before the deadline. Films will be screened and judged based on creativity, storytelling, cinematography, and impact.
