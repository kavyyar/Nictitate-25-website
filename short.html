Snap Shot saga

Capture moments and tell stories through your lens in photography!

Description:

Snapshot Saga is a photography event where participants of all skill levels can showcase their work, explore different styles, and connect with fellow photographers. It’s a great opportunity to gain inspiration, network, and celebrate creativity in photography.

Instructions:

  1. Each team is allowed to participate with one photographer.
  2. After the photo session, participants must send their best photographs to the event's WhatsApp group.
  3. The photo submissions must include the photographer’s name for identification
  4. All entries should be original and taken during the designated time frame.
