Word Snipper

Push yourself to think quickly , communicate effectively , and solve problems under time pressure.

Description:

Word Snipper is an engaging and dynamic game designed to test participants' vocabulary, creativity, and teamwork. Featuring two rounds of exciting challenges, the game pushes players to think quickly, communicate effectively, and solve problems under time pressure. With a focus on collaboration and strategy, Word Snipper offers a fun opportunity to showcase both individual skills and team coordination. Join in and put your word abilities to the test!

Instructions:

Round 1: Hangman Challenge

  1. One player guesses letters, while the other arranges them in the word.
  2. If a guessed letter is incorrect, parts of a stick-figure will be drawn.
  3. The game continues until the word is guessed or the figure is fully drawn.

Round 2: Art Blitz

  1. One player per team will be selected to draw.
  2. The other player must guess the word based on the drawing.
  3. The drawing cannot include letters.
  4. Scoring will be based on correct answers and time taken in both rounds.
  5. The team with the highest score after both rounds wins.
